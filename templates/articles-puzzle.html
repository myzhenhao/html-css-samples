Designing Delightful Dilemmas

Great puzzle design teaches without telling. It layers mechanics, rewards experimentation, and celebrates the "aha!" moment without punishing failure.

Teach, Twist, Master

Introduce a mechanic, remix it, then ask the player to demonstrate mastery — a classic cadence that never gets old.

Hint Economy

Offer optional nudges. Players feel smart when they choose to seek help, not when it’s forced.

Takeaways

  • Short loops, strong feedback
  • Clarity first, then style
  • Celebrate the solve

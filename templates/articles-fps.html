Precision Matters: Building an FPS Routine

Whether you main tac shooters or arena classics, consistency wins. This routine focuses on crosshair placement, recoil control, and movement discipline with short, repeatable drills.

Daily Drills

Start with 5 minutes of microflicks and 5 minutes of angle clearing. Follow with burst control on moving targets. Keep sensitivity fixed for two weeks before iterating.

Settings That Stick

Use raw input, low latency modes, and a frame cap that your rig can sustain. Prioritize clarity over eye-candy; clarity is a performance stat.

Takeaways

  • Warm-up: 10–15 mins max
  • Review one clutch or loss per session
  • Small wins scale — track them
